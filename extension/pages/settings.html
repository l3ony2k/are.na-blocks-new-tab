About this project

are.na blocks new tab swaps the default new tab for a curated grid of Are.na content. Each time you open a tab the extension randomly chooses from the cached blocks gathered from your configured channels and specific block IDs.

How sources work

Use channel slugs (the text after /channel/ in an Are.na URL) to pull in every block from a channel, and add individual block IDs (the number after /block/) when you want precise picks. The cache mixes everything together and the new tab view pulls a fresh selection on load.

New to Are.na?

Are.na is a creative research platform for collecting ideas, references, and projects. You can browse publicly, but creating a free account at are.na lets you follow channels, save blocks, and build your own collections.
